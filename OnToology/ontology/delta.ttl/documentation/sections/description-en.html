Instantiating DELTA ontology back to ToC

This section shows several example of instantiations of the DELTA ontology. Such example aims to illustrate how to describe (1) how to model a FEID, (2) how to model measurements and (3) how to model DR signals. The examples are graphically represented in Figure 3 and Figure 4.

The FEID information represented in the example 1 is the following:

Name: SmartHouse 01

IP: 160.40.45.105

Port: 8988

Longitude: 40.515673

Latitude:20.345897

Measurements:

  • Name: UpFlexibility
  • Feature of Interest: Room 1
  • Date: 20/08/2019
  • Value: 0.3 watts

The signal information represented in the example 2 is the following:

Source: HVAC 01

Owner: Consumer 01

oReward:

  • Type: Discount
  • Value: 13.5 euro
