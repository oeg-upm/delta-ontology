Instantiating DELTA ontology back to ToC

This section shows several example of instantiations of the DELTA ontology. Such example aims to illustrate how to describe (1) how to model a FEID, (2) how to model measurements and (3) how to model DR signals. The examples are graphically represented in Figure 3, Figure 4 and Figure 5 .

The FEID information represented in the example 1 is the following:

Name: FEID 01

IP: 160.40.45.105

Port: 8988

Longitude: 40.515673

Latitude:20.345897

oSector:

  • Name: Temperature
  • Area: http://www.opengis.net/def/crs/OGC/1.3/CRS84 POLYGON((21.5 18.5,23.5 18.5, 23.5 21,21.5 21,21.5 18.5))POLYGON((21.5 18.5,23.5 18.5, 23.5 21,21.5 21,21.5 18.5))

The properties information represented in the example 2 is the following:

Sensor: Sensor1

oMeasured Property:

  • Type: Flexibility
  • Value: 0.3 watt
  • Time stamp: 20-08-2019

The signal information represented in the example 3 is the following:

Source: DVN1

  • Interval: (2019-04-21T14:14:03.00 - 2019-04-21T14:14:03.00)
